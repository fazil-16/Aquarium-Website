Electric Green Glofish Tetra
GloFish Tetra is the latest selective-breeding. The genes of this tetra have been altered with the genes of certain anemones, corals or jellyfish that features the illuminescence, giving this type of tetra a bright, permanent neon hue. The coloration of the GloFish Tetra will not fade or change as it is within their genetic structure and will actually be passed on to their offspring while spawning. To see them "glow" at their best, keep under blue "actinic" lighting.
