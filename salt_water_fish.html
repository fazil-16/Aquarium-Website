Pragon Pipefish
Dragon pipefish is a tiny parasitic amphipod, a micro-amphipod which is 1/2 millimeters in length. It has a yellow body with a red abdomen and can eat anything from copepods, amphipods to Red Bugs. A dragon pipefish has a natural defense mechanism against red bugs and wasted colonies inside a well-established reef aquarium. It can be kept in a sea house tank with plentiful of rocks, caves, and overhangs for an environment that looks more like its natural habitat. The dragon pipefish is a peaceful fish and can sustain gentle tremors.
